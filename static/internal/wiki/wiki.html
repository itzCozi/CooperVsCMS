About CooperVsCMS

Bypass Restrictions, Explore Freely

Welcome to the CooperVsCMS home page! We believe in the power of free access to information and the unrestricted exploration of knowledge. Our unblocker service allows you to effectively break DNS filtering, enabling you to access the web without limitations.

Why Use Our Unblocker?

  • Unrestricted Access: CooperVsCMS does not and will not ever block any search / outgoing request.
  • Free Software: Feel safe knowing CooperVsCMS can be duplicated or updated at any time through its Github
  • Seamless Experience: Experience a hassle-free and familiar browsing experience with our "Imitation Chrome" tabs.

How It Works

CooperVsCMS's unblocker service employs an advanced proxy to circumvent network restrictions. That proxy is called UltraViolet (UV) made by Titanium Network for the sole purpose of liberating restricted content.

Incognito / Anonymous Authentication

Anonymous authentication is a feature of Firebase that I implemented. It serves as an option for users who may be too lazy to create an account. Instead, I decided to provide an easier and faster way to log in or create an account. To prevent abuse, I added a 12-hour cooldown period, allowing only two anonymous accounts to be created per user per day.

Create an Account
  1. Head to the incognito login page
  2. Input YOUR full name
  3. Create a display name
  4. Welcome to CooperVsCMS

Login to an Account

  1. Head to the incognito login page
  2. Enter the exact same credentials you created your account with
  3. Welcome to CooperVsCMS

Email Login Authentication

Email login is also a feature of Firebase, this is the recommended way of using CooperVsCMS due to ease of use and the average user's familiarity with a simple email address and password system.

Create an Account
  1. Head to the register page
  2. Input YOUR first and last name
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Create a password
  5. Welcome to CooperVsCMS

Login to an Account

  1. Head to the login page
  2. Enter your email
  3. Enter your password
  4. Welcome to CooperVsCMS

F.A.Q.

CooperVsCMS itself is blocked?
Unlucky. I havent found a way to get around this but follow this for more help.

Access Denied?
CooperVsCMS sometimes denies access to users not on ChromeOS... access it on your chromebook.

I can't login?
Well you could have gotten booted or you forgot your credentials, look here for more.

Does it unblock youtube videos?
Yes. CooperVsCMS unblocks all youtube videos once visiting it on the unblocker site.

Why is it running slow?
Proxy's are usually slow but turning off the intensive background may help.

How do I disable the background?
Click the three dots next to the address bar and click "Change background".

How to skip youtube ads?
Click the three dots next to the address bar and click "Skip YT AD".

What happens to my account data?
All data provided during login & registration is saved to a Firestore database.

Restrictive Actions

What are restrictive actions?
Restrictive actions are actions carried out by administrators to restrict users in CooperVsCMS's case that includes: removing accounts, altering credentials and throttling accounts.

Removing Accounts

Removal of your account means your info leads me to suspect your "undesirable" or your usage is high (like really high), contact me and we can resolve the issue.

Altering Credentials

Altering of your accounts credentials is due to invalid email address or restricting access from the user of the account, again reach out to me to resolve the issue and get your account returned.

Throttling Accounts

I barely do this throttling is when I limit the amount of requests and strenuous things you can do to save resources.

Other CMS Annoyances

Other ways to bypass restrictions?

Well CMS also has a program called "ZScaler" that blocks traffic to apps like snapchat, simply use your hotspot or your friends hotspots are completely unrestricted.

My hotspot is so slow?

This could be because a lot of other people are using there hotspot too (likely) or your school is jamming it (very unlikely) which is against the Federal Communications Commission rule on "Jammer Enforcement".

What the fuck is ZScaler?

ZScaler is a cloud based proxy that runs (usually) at router level, but there is hope, (and more hope) IF you have local admin rights and ZScaler is implemented at client level.
