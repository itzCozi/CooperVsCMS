My Stance on Internet Censorship

When we limit what people can share or see, not only does it discourage thinking outside of the box, but it can also cause a major inconvenience to the user and limits freedom of speech. Not only do all three of these things go against libertarianism, a movement that seeks to maximize autonomy and political freedom for its followers, but this also has a lot to do with modern internet censorship.
